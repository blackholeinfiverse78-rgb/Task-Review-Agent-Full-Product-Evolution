## 1. Executive Quality & Readiness Dashboard
This screen provides executive-level visibility into compliance metrics, product readiness scores, and open risks across the active ecosystem.

![Parikshak Executive Dashboard](file:///g:/Live%20Task%20Review%20Agent%20-%202/review_packets/code_review/parikshak_dashboard.png)

### Key Telemetry Displayed:
* **Compliance Level**: 100% compliant builds running under live load.
* **Dimensions Monitored**: Automated green/red indicator checks for Runtime, Observability, Replayability, Provenance, Security, Versioning, Recovery, and Governance.
* **Ecosystem Map**: Connected adapters for Gov-OS, Saarthi, Bucket, Pravah, and Niyantran.
* **Risk Register**: Live log highlighting blockages or pending human-in-loop escalations.
